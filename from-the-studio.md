## When the free pass is not enough

`double-text-risk` is meant to catch one follow-up mistake before the user needs a fuller read of the thread or person.

It also maps cleanly to recruiter, client, support, family, cofounder, vendor, sales, and workplace follow-up loops where one extra ping can hurt leverage more than help it.

The paid next steps are explicit:

- `Deep Dive` — `$19 once` for one loaded thread or one recurring person
- `Starter` — `$14.99/mo` for repeat message reads
- `Practitioner` — `$99.99/mo` for coach, recruiter, mediator, trainer, support lead, or client-facing team workflows
