double-text-risk is better when

  • you need a clean send now / wait / switch channel / do not send call
  • the issue is one drafted follow-up in dating, recruiter, client, support, family, sales, or workplace context
  • you do not want mood or prompt wording to steer the answer

general AI is better when

  • you want rewrites, brainstorming, or roleplay
  • you need broad drafting help after the decision is already clear
  • you accept less stable answers in exchange for breadth
